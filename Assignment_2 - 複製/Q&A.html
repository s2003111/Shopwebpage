Q&A

Q1.How to confirm my order?

The advertisements on the Site are invitations to you to make offers to purchase products and services on the Site and are not offers to sell. AVShop acceptance of an order will be deemed once a shipping notice has been dispatched to you via email.

Q2.How to pay?

AVShop accepts Visa, MasterCard, American Express and PayPal. Please also ask about Interac Email Money Transfers, Wire Transfers, School Board Purchase Orders, or Leasing options.

Q3.How to ship my goods?

AVShop will ship through service providing by SFExpress. The delivery cost is included in the product price.

Q4.Can I return the goods?

All returned opened items are subject to a maximum 25% restocking fee. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, returns will be accepted for refund, exchange or store credit (less restocking fee & shipping) within 30 days of invoice date. AVShop will accept returns for defective goods that you have ordered from us within 30 days of invoice date for exchange only.

Q5.Can I enjoy warranty?

AVShop is an authorized retailer for each of the brands available on our website. As such, your purchase is authorized for the manufacturer's warranty in Hong Kong.
